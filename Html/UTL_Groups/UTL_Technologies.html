UTL Technologies Overview

Established in 2001UTL Technologies Ltd. is a premier institute located in Bangalore, Karnataka. The institute offers Degree, Certificate courses including 2 PG programs. These programs are offered in Full Time mode. Candidate can pursue multiple ranges of courses like Certificate, M.E./M.Tech in the stream of IT & Software, Engineering. Over the period, UTL Technologies Ltd. has gained expertise through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Embedded Systems & VLSI. Courses offered by the institute are in the fee range of INR 136,940. The institute provides good facilities all around the campus.

Vision

To be a global leader in producing industry ready manpower perfectly understanding the importance of imparting the conceptual knowledge and also the need for relating it to the current technology trends.

Mission

To be a Premier Institute with continuous improvement and achieve excellent customer satisfaction in providing high end skill training and development on emerging technologies by Bridging the Gap between Academics and Industry in the fields of Information Technology and Communication
