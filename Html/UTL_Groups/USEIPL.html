USEIPL Overview

United Sustainable Energy India Pvt. Ltd.(USEIPL) is an Independent Power Producer (IPP), engaged in the business of power generation from renewable energy sources. USEIPL is a part of UTL Group which has established its footprint in varied business areas ranging from Telecom, Mobile Handsets, Software solutions, e-governance, Real Estate and Power. USEIPL has been at the forefront of India's drive to deliver clean and affordable electricity to its consumers, thereby fostering its endeavor towards energy independence. USEIPL focuses on clean energy technologies such as Wind and Solar currently as it develops, builds, owns and operates large scale renewable projects across various states in India.

We currently have an installed capacity of 100MW of Wind and 6MW of Solar projects in operation, with a further pipeline of over 200MW capacity under various stages of development. Our endeavor is to continue this sustainable growth towards achieving over 1GW capacity of renewable energy projects in the near future.

Vision

To be a global leader in renewable energy sector empowering green initiatives through its eco-sustainable ventures.

Mission

To develop renewable energy projects, using future ready technologies, in a manner that is socially, environmentally and economically sustainable. while adhering to the best practices of corporate governance for the protection of the interests of stakeholders; providing a professionally dynamic environment to our employees, and work efficiently and diligently to the utmost satisfaction of our valued clients.
