Trigyn Overview

Founded in 1986, Trigyn Technologies, Inc. has grown to become a leader in helping organizations of all sizes build a sustainable competitive advantage using technology. Today, Trigyn is a global provider of digital transformation solutions and services that help improve the lives of more than two billion people around the world today.

Our success is built on our our unwavering focus on quality service delivery, and providing excellent value to our customers. Commitment to quality at Trigyn is not only phrase but a way of life. Trigyn holds many externally-audited quality management certifications including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 27001:2013, ISO 20000:2018, ISO 14001:2015 and CMMI Ver 2.0 DEV Maturity Level-5.

With more than 2,500 experienced professionals deployed, and active engagements in more than 27 countries spanning 5 continents, Trigyn is always nearby.

Trigyn is your trusted technology advisor. With a large team of qualified professionals, Trigyn is always read to help. Whether you need a few resources to complement your project team, experienced advisors to help you solve a challenging business problem, or a full project team to deliver and end-to-end solution, we have experienced experts who are ready for the task.
